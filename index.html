Why CubicPostcode?

  • ✅ 1 cubic meter precision — truly 3D addressing
  • ✅ Completely free and open source (no proprietary fees)
  • ✅ Works completely offline
  • ✅ Designed for social good, emergency services, logistics, and built environment
  • ✅ Helps communities in poverty by improving location-based services

How It Works

Converts GPS (lat, lon, alt) → ECEF coordinates → unique 22-digit cube identifier.

Perfect for the built environment, digital twins, and inclusive geospatial tools.
